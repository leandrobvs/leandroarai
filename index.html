Hello, my name is Leandro Arai, and I have a degree in Architecture and Urbanism. Despite my initial education, I have always been close to technology, both at work and during leisure time. Now, I am in a career transition, combining my passion for data analysis and problem-solving with a new professional journey in the field of Business Intelligence (BI) and Data.

Inspirado pela frase de Einstein, acredito que, assim como o universo segue leis e padrões, os dados que geramos diariamente também carregam informações valiosas que podem ser transformadas em decisões estratégicas. Inspired by Einstein's quote, I believe that, just as the universe follows laws and patterns, the data we generate daily also carries valuable information that can be transformed into strategic decisions. This perspective has guided my transition into the field of technology, where I aim to use my natural curiosity and analytical skills to drive meaningful results.

Minha jornada começou de forma despretensiosa, com perguntas do dia a dia sobre probabilidades e padrões, mas evoluiu para um profundo interesse por estatísticas e análise de dados. My journey began unassumingly, with everyday questions about probabilities and patterns, but it evolved into a deep interest in statistics and data analysis. Today, I am focused on developing solutions that transform raw data into actionable insights, adding value to businesses and building a future based on informed decisions.

Today, I am fully dedicated to the field of data analysis, with experience in using techniques such as ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) and EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) to transform data into strategic information. My approach combines modern tools such as SQL, Power BI, and Excel.

Tenho paixão por explorar dados e criar soluções que otimizem processos e tragam resultados tangíveis. Acredito que dados são mais do que números: são histórias esperando para serem contadas.
